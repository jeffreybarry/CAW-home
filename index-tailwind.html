Cares about Wildlife

Know a kid who loves learning about animals?

Cares About Wildlife is a self-paced online biodiversity curriculum aligned with the Common Core Standards.

Crafted for grades 4-8, Cares About Wildlife helps young minds grow by taking kids on an educational journey into the habitats of the gray wolf.

Through learning activities, students build an interactive learning notebook showcasing their knowledge about wildlife.

Beta access will launch later in 2021. Progress updates on Twitter.
